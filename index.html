The F.I.T.T. Formula

Key factors in determining how much physical activity is enough.

F

Frequency

How often you do physical activity. (e.g., Daily, 3x a week)

I

Intensity

How hard you perform. Determined by type and goals.

T

Time

How long you do the activity. (e.g., 15-30 mins min)

T

Type

The specific activity chosen to build specific fitness.
